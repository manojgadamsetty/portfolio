About Me

As a Staff Software Engineer with 9+ years of experience, I specialize in the intersection of mobile application development and cybersecurity. My core mission is to build secure, high-performance mobile applications that protect user data, meet compliance standards, and defend against evolving security threats.

I bring 9+ years of iOS development experience using Swift and Objective-C, and over 3.5 years of Android experience with Java and Kotlin. Throughout my career, I've led and contributed to the development of multiple mobile applications with security integrated from the ground up adopting Secure Software Development Life Cycle (SSDLC) practices.

A strong advocate for mobile app security, I have hands-on experience conducting threat modeling, vulnerability assessments, code reviews, and penetration testing aligned with OWASP Mobile Top 10. I actively integrate DevSecOps principles into mobile CI/CD pipelines, ensuring security is foundational to the development process.
