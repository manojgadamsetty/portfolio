About Me

Hello! I'm Manoj Gadamsetty, a Staff Software Engineer based in Hyderabad, India, with 10+ years of experience at the intersection of mobile development and cybersecurity. I'm passionate about building secure, scalable solutions that protect millions of users worldwide while maintaining exceptional user experience.

Currently at Palo Alto Networks, I lead mobile security initiatives for the next-generation enterprise identity platform with Workforce Password Manager, pioneering secure architectures for password management with offline data management, autofill capabilities, and watch app integration. I specialize in advanced authentication, zero-trust access, IAM, SSO, and enterprise-grade security with primary focus on secure credential management. My work directly powers enterprise rollouts and reduces risk exposure for high-value customers. With a customer-first approach, I excel in enterprise product environments where SLA management and rapid issue resolution are critical. I've mastered navigating complex policy landscapes and configuration challenges to adapt applications for diverse enterprise needs. My expertise includes conducting over 150 root cause analyses (RCAs) for reported issues, ensuring robust solutions that maintain service reliability and user trust. Over the course of my career, I've led the development and successful launch of several high-impact iOS applications that have collectively served millions of users. These apps have maintained an impressive 99.99% crash-free stability rate through comprehensive testing strategies, performance optimization, and proactive monitoring. This reliability, combined with intuitive user interfaces and robust functionality, has resulted in consistently high app store ratings and positive user feedback.

My technical toolkit spans iOS (Swift, Objective-C), Android (Kotlin, Java), macOS, secure backend development (.NET, Python, Golang), and full-stack DevSecOps. I have hands-on experience implementing iXGuard obfuscation and runtime app self-protection (RASP) for enterprise iOS apps. I've also conducted cross-platform POCs using React Native, Kotlin Multiplatform (KMM), and Flutter for enterprise evaluation. I specialize in threat modeling, security architecture design, code reviews, identifying and resolving critical security vulnerabilities, and incident response. I'm deeply committed to secure software development lifecycle (SSDLC) best practices and regularly mentor senior engineers on security-first development approaches. Currently exploring LLMs and building RAG systems for intelligent developer tooling.

Beyond coding, I value open communication, believe in lifelong learning, and thrive on solving complex problems that matter. I'm passionate about mentoring talented teams, driving innovation in mobile security, and creating products that users can trust. Whether guiding technical deep-dives, presenting to business stakeholders, or collaborating with cross-functional teams, I bring clarity, passion, and an unwavering commitment to user safety and privacy.
