About Me

Hello! I'm Manoj Gadamsetty, a Principal Software Engineer based in Hyderabad, India, with 10+ years of experience building secure, scalable systems across mobile, identity, and workflow automation. I focus on delivering AI-native engineering systems that improve velocity without sacrificing reliability or governance.

Currently at Palo Alto Networks, I work on secure identity and delivery workflows that combine observability, automation, and AI-assisted analysis. I specialize in advanced authentication, zero-trust access, enterprise identity, and operational systems that make product teams faster and more effective, with endpoint development focused primarily on iOS, iPadOS, and macOS apps.

Over the course of my career, I've led products and platforms that serve large user bases with a strong focus on reliability, automation, and measurable engineering outcomes. I prefer systems that are auditable, testable, and simple to evolve.

My Journey

I started my career at BRN Infotech as an iOS intern, building a strong foundation in Swift and Objective-C. I progressed through Proxim Quest IT Solutions and Edwisely, where I developed secure educational applications with LaTeX/KaTeX rendering. At Frost Interactive, I built production video platforms with custom AVPlayer controls and FairPlay DRM licensing.

My enterprise journey deepened at CyberArk, where I spent 4.5+ years architecting privileged access and identity systems. That work shaped my understanding of OAuth 2.0, OIDC, SAML, FIDO2, and enterprise identity federation.

Today, at Palo Alto Networks, I’m focused on secure platform delivery, workflow automation, and identity systems that support enterprise security requirements while improving engineering throughput.

Technical Toolkit

My technical toolkit spans iOS (Swift, Objective-C), Android (Kotlin, Java), macOS, and secure backend development (.NET, Python, Golang) with full-stack DevSecOps capabilities. I also work with MCP orchestration, RAG systems, Skills Builder and multi-agent orchestration automation, and AI-assisted refactoring. I specialize in threat modeling, security architecture design, code reviews, and incident response.
