About Me

Hello! I'm Manoj Gadamsetty, a Staff Software Engineer based in Hyderabad, India, with 10+ years of experience at the intersection of mobile development and cybersecurity. I'm passionate about building secure, scalable solutions that protect millions of users worldwide while maintaining exceptional user experience.

Currently at Palo Alto Networks, I lead mobile security initiatives for the next-generation enterprise identity platform with Workforce Password Manager, pioneering secure architectures for password management with offline data management, autofill capabilities, and watch app integration. I specialize in advanced authentication, zero-trust access, IAM, SSO, and enterprise-grade security with primary focus on secure credential management. My work directly powers enterprise rollouts and reduces risk exposure for high-value customers. With a customer-first approach, I excel in enterprise product environments where SLA management and rapid issue resolution are critical. I've mastered navigating complex policy landscapes and configuration challenges to adapt applications for diverse enterprise needs. My expertise includes conducting over 150 root cause analyses (RCAs) for reported issues, ensuring robust solutions that maintain service reliability and user trust.

Over the course of my career, I've led the development and successful launch of several high-impact iOS applications that have collectively served millions of users. These apps have maintained an impressive 99.99% crash-free stability rate through comprehensive testing strategies, performance optimization, and proactive monitoring. This reliability, combined with intuitive user interfaces and robust functionality, has resulted in consistently high app store ratings and positive user feedback.

My Journey

I started my career at BRN Infotech as an iOS intern, building a strong foundation in Swift and Objective-C. I progressed through Proxim Quest IT Solutions and Edwisely, where I developed secure educational applications with LaTeX/KaTeX mathematical rendering. At Frost Interactive, I built production video platforms with custom AVPlayer controls and FairPlay DRM licensing.

My enterprise journey truly began at CyberArk, where I spent 4.5+ years architecting privilege access management (PAM) solutions and building identity platforms serving 100,000+ users. This experience shaped my deep understanding of OAuth 2.0, OIDC, SAML, FIDO2 authentication, MDM profiles, and enterprise identity federation.

Today, at Palo Alto Networks, I'm leading the development of enterprise identity platforms that power next-generation authentication and zero-trust architecture. I work on implementing OAuth 2.0, OIDC, SAML 2.0, FIDO2/WebAuthn, and building secure mobile applications that enterprises trust with their most sensitive operations.

Technical Toolkit

My technical toolkit spans iOS (Swift, Objective-C), Android (Kotlin, Java), macOS, and secure backend development (.NET, Python, Golang) with full-stack DevSecOps capabilities. Currently exploring LLMs and building RAG systems for intelligent developer tooling.
